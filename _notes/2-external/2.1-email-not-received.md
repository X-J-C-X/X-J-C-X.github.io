# 2.1 Email Not Received

## Issue Summary

Customer reports not receiving expected email.

## Response Template

```
Hello [Customer Name],

Thank you for contacting us about the missing email.

To help investigate this issue, please provide:

1. Sender's email address
2. Approximate date and time the email was sent
3. Subject line of the email
4. Any error message the sender may have received

I will check our logs and get back to you within [timeframe].

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Support Team
```

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. **Check spam/junk folder**
2. **Verify email address** - Check for typos
3. **Check mail rules** - Auto-filing rules
4. **Message trace** - Search in mail logs
5. **Check sender authentication** - SPF/DKIM/DMARC

## Internal Notes

- Log ticket number
- Check message trace first
- Escalate if not found in 24h
